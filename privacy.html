Overview

Daylight is a local-first Android app for activity logging, exercise logging, journaling, and personal reflection.

Daylight is designed so that your data stays on your device by default. Daylight does not provide cloud sync, cloud backup, user accounts, hosted storage, advertising, or third-party analytics.

Data stored by the app

Daylight may store the following data locally on your device:

  • activity categories and activities
  • activity intervals and timestamps
  • exercise names, sets, weights, reps, and dates
  • journal questions and journal entries
  • app settings and export settings
  • export history and export status information

This data is used to provide the app’s core logging, history, export, scheduling, and reflection features.

Local storage

Daylight stores app data locally on your device using Android app storage. If you delete the app or clear app data, your local Daylight data may be deleted unless you have exported it separately.

Daylight does not operate a server that stores your app data.

Manual and scheduled file exports

Daylight allows you to export your data to a ZIP file containing CSV files and metadata. You control where this export file is saved.

If you save an export to a folder, cloud drive, backup folder, or other storage provider, that destination may be controlled by another app or service. Daylight does not control those external storage locations.

HTTPS endpoint export

Daylight includes an optional HTTPS endpoint export feature.

If you enable endpoint export, Daylight sends a ZIP export of your app data to the HTTPS endpoint URL that you configure. This export may include activity logs, exercise logs, journal entries, custom questions, timestamps, and metadata.

Only configure an endpoint that you own or trust. Daylight does not control the endpoint you provide and cannot control what happens to your data after it is sent.

Disabling endpoint export stops future endpoint exports but does not delete data that has already been sent to an endpoint.

Network use

Daylight does not require internet access for normal local logging. Network access is only used for features that require it, such as optional HTTPS endpoint export.

Data sharing

Daylight does not sell your data.

Daylight does not share your data with the developer, advertisers, analytics providers, or a Daylight-operated cloud service.

If you enable HTTPS endpoint export, your export data is sent to the endpoint that you configure. This is user-directed sharing to a destination chosen by you.

Security

Endpoint exports require an HTTPS URL. HTTPS protects data in transit between your device and the configured endpoint, but it does not guarantee that the endpoint itself is trustworthy or securely managed.

You are responsible for choosing secure export destinations and protecting exported files.

Children

Daylight is not intended for children. The app is intended for personal use by adults and general users who want to record and reflect on their own activity, exercise, and journal data.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If the app’s data practices change materially, the policy should be updated before or at the same time as the app change.
